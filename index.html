Dynamite Roll

A dynamite roll is a type of Western-style sushi.
It contains a piece of prawn tempura and masago
(capelin roe), with vegetables like radish sprouts,
avocado and/or cucumber, as well as Japanese
mayonnaise.

California Roll

A California roll or California maki is a makizushi
sushi roll that is usually rolled inside-out, and
containing cucumber, crab, and avocado.

Crunch Roll

Crunch Roll is 100% tender white meat slowly
cooked in our spicy Buffalo sauce and then mixed
with a special blend of cheeses. Each one is then
individually hand wrapped in a crispy shell and
coated with our secret recipe breading to provide
its unique CRUNCH!
